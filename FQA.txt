1. What is Node ?
Nodes are building block of Godot, almost everything is made out of nodes. There are different 
types of nodes, each with a specific function. Node that shows image of player, 
or show text on the screen.

2. What is Scene?
Scenes is just a bunch of nodes grouped together, like player and level.

Assets are individual components that make game world, they are music,sprites,animations
